Tree of Knowledge

advanced simulation modelling

universal knowledge repository

Create the most accurate and realistic models of the world to solve your problems!
With Tree of Knowledge you can easily create an accurate simulation model of your system, by applying already simulated real-world behaviour. The simulation models are optimized and verified with extensive real world data from an integrated, holistic knowledge-base. The resulting models are more accurate and more informative than machine learning models, statistical models and simulation models.
